Media Services Description
Video Video services can include anything from on-site filming to editing, graphics, image and sound design, special effects and more. I like to offer a wide range of videography skills that can meet the needs of any client.
Photo Photography services include portrait photography, event photography, product photography, corporate photography, sports photography, pet photography, and more. All services also include photo editing, library orginization, and the finished medium(s) of choice.
Graphic Design As a digital media specialist I offer a wide range of graphic design services tailored for the digital world. I can create visuals for social media, websites, infographics, news segments, print media, presentations, and more. I create these designs to match the client's brand and messaging and produce noticable results.
Web Design & Development I provide web design and development services to create and enhance websites. In development I strive to design user-friendly and visually appealing websites, ensuring a seamless user experience. I can handle the technical aspects, such as coding and functionality, to make sure the website runs smoothly and performs well, as well as the design elements to make a smooth and engaging site.
